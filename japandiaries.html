dear japan...

I have been studying the Japanese language since 9th grade. It is actually my 3rd language after Korean, which I studied on my own (and can now barely speak). Originally, I always wanted to study Korean in a proper educational setting, but upon entering university, I found myself already too deep into Japanese.

In 2017, I travelled to Japan for the first time with my high school Japanese language teacher and a small group of classmates. That trip changed my life; I fell in love with the beautiful country and have become interested in working there professionally in the future. Since then, I have been keen on returning to Japan, and I did two years later.

But another two weeks wasn't enough. I dream of one day visiting all 47 prefectures (similar to states or provinces) of Japan, but if two weeks could only cover a small part of Tokyo, how long would such a journey last?

Follow my journey through this online documentation of my travels to Japan!
